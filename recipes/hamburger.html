Hamburger

Description

A delicious hamburger to enjoy alone or with family.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Hamburger
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Brown the hamburger and put the cheese
  2. Cut the Bread
  3. In the bread base, add Hamburger, Tomato, Lettuce, Onion, Pickles
  4. Close with the upper bread
