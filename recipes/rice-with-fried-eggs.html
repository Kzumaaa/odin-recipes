Rice with fried eggs

Description

A delicious rice with fried eggs dish to enjoy alone or with family.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare a pot and place half a cup of rice
  2. For every half cup of rice, add a cup of boiling water.
  3. Then leave the pot on low heat.
  4. Then fry 2 eggs in a pan
  5. Finally serve everything on a plate and enjoy.
